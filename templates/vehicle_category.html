About Vehicle Category Detection

Vehicle Category Detection is crucial for traffic management and road safety. By identifying and categorizing vehicles, it helps in:

  • Assisting in urban planning and infrastructure development.

Our system uses advanced deep learning models to accurately detect and categorize vehicles, ensuring efficient traffic management and safer roads for everyone.
